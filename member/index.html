about

Expandables is a series of cute on-chain generated pixel pandas. Adopt, breed and venture your way into the Metaverse using our ERC-20 token $BAMBOO and the upcoming Panda Voxels! Our project is centered around the thrilling experience of being part of the Metaverse and introducing plenty of pandas to their new habitat. Adopting a panda doesn't only mean you're able to breed the cutest babies and show off your voxel in the Metaverse, you'll also be helping us to fight the endangerment of this beautiful species in real life!

frequently asked questions

What does term "on chain" mean?

It means the NFTs are generated and stored on the blockchain, no external servers are used. Which means your NFT's availability is ensured as long as the Ethereum blockchain exists.
How are pandas generated and stored?

Panda's traits are assigned randomly during mint and stored on the Ethereum blockchain. Images will also be stored on Arweave on chain, so you will always be able access them. We also use Chainlink VRF to provide 100% randomness for the generation.
Is Expandables supporting charities?

10 eth will be donated to an organization after launch, yet to be chosen. There are only around 2,300 pandas left in the world, and we will be happy to support the difficult mission of breeding pandas.
Is there going to be a community wallet?

Yes! To start with, 10% of our primary sales will be deposited in our community wallet. Besides that, it will receive 1% of secondary sales. These funds will be used to keep expanding the Expandables.
What will be the royalties fee on the secondary market?

We have decided to set a royalties fee of 5%.
What is $BAMBOO?

$BAMBOO is an ERC-20 utility token generated by the genesis pandas, this token's first utility will be to enable breeding baby pandas.
How is $BAMBOO generated?

By staking a genesis panda it will produce $BAMBOO. Each panda produces 10 $BAMBOO per day. You can unstake your panda at anytime.
What is the max supply of $BAMBOO?

The max supply of $BAMBOO is 10 million tokens. The staking ends when all $BAMBOO is mined which means that no more tokens can be generated. The more genesis pandas staked, the faster we reach the max supply cap.
What can I do with $BAMBOO?

Breed the cutest baby pandas or choose to name your genesis panda by burning your $BAMBOO tokens. You will need 1,000 $BAMBOO and two genesis pandas to breed a baby panda, but only 50 $BAMBOO to name your genesis panda.
What can I do with my voxel pandas?
